The Angel Orchestra was founded in 1990 and is now a well-established and thriving orchestra. Peter Fender is our inspirational music director and conductor and has conducted the orchestra since 2008. The orchestra has participated in many exciting projects including exchange trips with an Italian orchestra, improvisation at the ICA and performing live the Laura Rossi score for the Battle of the Somme film.

We welcome newcomers to our rehearsals and our social events.
