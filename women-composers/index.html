Supporting women composers

We have historically played some music by women composers, and the positive feedback from our Votes for Women centenary concert in 2018 encouraged us to decide on a policy of playing one piece by a woman composer in every concert for our 2019/20 season. We are continuing with this policy for our 2022 season.

We are grateful for the continuing support of the Ambache Charitable Trust, which is active in raising the profile of women composers.
