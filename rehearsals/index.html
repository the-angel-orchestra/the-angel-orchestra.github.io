Rehearsals

We rehearse during term time on Wednesdays from 7.30pm to 9.30pm in St Silas Church on Risinghill Street, London N1 9UL. Most of our concerts are at St Silas Church as well.

The last evening rehearsal before the concert is usually until 10pm, but there will also be one final rehearsal on concert day, which will start at 2pm or 3pm depending on the concert start time.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions per term are £90, with a concessionary rate of £50, and can be paid via our account on the Local Giving website, or bank transfer to 60-12-02 / 14045222. Local Giving is preferred as it immediately takes care of Gift Aid.

Mailing List

If you are in the orchestra you can join the orchestra mailing list to get the latest information about rehearsals. Send an email to players-join@the-angel-orchestra.co.uk to join the mailing list.

To get in contact with the orchestra please send an email to Matt Porter on info@the-angel-orchestra.co.uk
