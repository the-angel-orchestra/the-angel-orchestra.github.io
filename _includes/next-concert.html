Beethoven Fundraising Weekend Workshop: 28 and 29 January 2023

In just one weekend, we will rehearse and then perform Beethoven's 9th Symphony, to raise funds for the orchestra and for Apollo Music Projects, a charity that aims to bring live classical music to children who might not otherwise experience it.

For more details, and especially if you are interested in playing or singing, please email the orchestra: info@the-angel-orchestra.co.uk.

The performance will be at 5.30pm on Sunday, 29 January at St Silas Church on Risinghill Street, N1 9UL.
